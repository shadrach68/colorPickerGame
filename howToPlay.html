How to play the game

Choosing Colors

  • 1,2,3 = RED;
  • 4,5,6 = YELLOW;
  • 7,8,9,10 = BLUE;

After you input any of the color numbers, Click on the "Guess" button.

Rules of the game

  1. After choosing a color and clicking on the Guess button , wait until the dialog box disappears before you guess again.
  2. Once you have guessed the color correctly, then you would have to click on the "Reset" button to play again.
  3. Make sure to input all of the color numbers EX("if you want to guess red you input, 1,2,3."), Do not omit anyone.
