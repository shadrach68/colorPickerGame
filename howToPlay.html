About

This game is all about checking and testing how good you are at guessing. If you get the congratulations at the end of the five trials, then you are a really good guesser!

How to play the game

The game consist of five (5) trials/levels. So for you to win , you would have to get three or more guesses correctly.

Choosing Colors

  • 1 = Red;
  • 2 = Green;
  • 3 = Pink;
  • 4 = Yellow;
  • 5 = Orange;
  • 6 = Purple;
  • 7 = Blue;
  • 8 = Black;
  • 9 = White;
  • 10 = Golden;

After you input any of the color numbers, Click on the "Guess" button.

Rules of the game

  1. After choosing a color and clicking on the Guess button , wait until the dialog box disappears before you guess again.
  2. You would have to guess five (5) times for you to complete the game.
  3. You either get a success dialog or a Sorry dialog , depending on how well or bad you guessed.
  4. Note: You Can only input one (1) digit and You can't input any other thing aside from the numbers given to the colors.
